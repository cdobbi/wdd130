Rafting Site Plan

Christi Dobbins

Overview

The Rafting Site is a website that provides information about rafting trips in Utah. The site will include information about the rivers, the rapids, and the outfitters that provide guided trips. The target audience for the Rafting Site is people who are interested in rafting in Utah.

Purpose

The purpose of the Rafting Site is to provide information about our business which is, rafting trips in Utah. The site will include information about the rivers used, the rapids, the outfitters that provide guided trips, pricing, group packages, etc., safety, and what to expect.

Target Audience

The target audience for the business Rafting Site is people who are interested in outdoor activities specifically, rafting in Utah. This includes families, groups, and individuals who are looking for a fun and exciting outdoor experience.
