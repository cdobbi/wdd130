Utah | USA

Utah is the Beehive state located western United States. It became the 45th state admitted to the U.S. on January 4, 1896. Utah has a population of more than 3 million. Urban development is mostly concentrated in two areas: the Wasatch Front and Washington County. Utah is bordered by Colorado, Wyoming, Idaho, Arizona, Nevada and a tiny point of New Mexico in the southeast.
